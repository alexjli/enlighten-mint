Enlighten-mint

A vocab app that helps you remember everyday words

Highlight a word and right click to "Enlighten" it and search it's definition and relavant words!
These words are then stored at the link below for you to view and study later.

See all stored words

The words are then stored and can also be accessed at anytime from the side panel. The side panel can be accessed by clicking the edge of the panel peaking out of the edge or clicking the "E" at the top right of your screen.
